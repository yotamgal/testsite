Rabbits: Fascinating Creatures

Rabbits are small mammals in the family Leporidae. They are known for their long ears, fluffy tails, and strong hind legs. Rabbits are herbivores, meaning they primarily eat plants such as grass, vegetables, and hay.

These animals are highly social and often live in groups. They are also known for their ability to reproduce quickly, which has made them a symbol of fertility in many cultures.

Rabbits are popular pets due to their gentle nature and adorable appearance. However, they require proper care, including a balanced diet, regular exercise, and a safe environment to thrive.
